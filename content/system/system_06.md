## S.6 Verification and acceptance criteria
- **[S.6.1] Response Time Verification:** The system shall be tested to ensure it meets the 5s (fire), 10s (intrusion), and immediate (medical) thresholds.  
- **[S.6.2] Reliability Verification:** The system must demonstrate <1% false alarm rate over a year.  
- **[S.6.3] Availability Verification:** The system must be verified for 100% uptime.
