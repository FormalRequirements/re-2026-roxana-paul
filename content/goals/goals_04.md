## G.4 Functionality overview
The system performs the following high-level functions:  
- **[G.4.1] Event Monitoring:** Detects intrusions, fires, and medical emergencies.  
- **[G.4.2] Alerting:** Automatically contacts the relevant emergency service upon detection.  
- **[G.4.3] Integration:** Connects with smart home devices for automated responses and security cameras for verification.  
- **[G.4.4] Multi-location Support:** Supports monitoring for multiple homes or properties for a single user.
