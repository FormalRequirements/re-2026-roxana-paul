## G.5 High-level usage scenarios
- **[G.5.1] Scenario 1 (Medical):** A user wearing the physical controller experiences a fall. The system detects the medical emergency and immediately alerts the ambulance service.  
- **[G.5.2] Scenario 2 (Intrusion):** The system detects an intrusion. Within 10 seconds, the system alerts the police and uses security cameras for visual verification.
