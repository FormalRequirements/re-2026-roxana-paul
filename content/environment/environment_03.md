## E.3 Constraints
The environment imposes the following strict constraints on the system:
* **Uptime:** The system must maintain 100% uptime to ensure it is operational 24/7.
* **Response Time (Intrusion):** Maximum 10 seconds to detect and alert.
* **Response Time (Fire):** Maximum 5 seconds to detect and alert.
* **Response Time (Medical):** Immediate response required.
